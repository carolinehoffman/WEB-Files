CU Hiking Club

About

The CU Hiking Club is a group of hikers, backpackers and adventure-lovers that come together to explore the

areas around Boulder! We lead trips every weekend of various difficulty and hold weekly social hours for members

to get together and socialize.

How to Join

First, sign up for our email list! Almost all of our information is communicated via our weekly emails.

Then, stop by our UMC office, during Office Hours to sign the membership waiver and pay your dues.

You can sign up for our email list by emailing sympa@lists.colorado.edu.

You'll leave the subject line blank. And in the body of the message, type:

"subscribe cuhc Your Full Name"

Example:

subscribe cuhc Caroline Hoffman

Our Officers

The club is run by three Executive Officers who handle administrative tasks, club organization, and outreach.

Officers organize the weekly trips that the club runs... including food, logistics, rides, and gear, while being

sure that everyone on the trip has an awesome time! All of our officers have some level of medical training, whether

that's WFR, WFA, WEMT, or basic first aid. We work together to make sure your trip is fun, safe and memorable!

Our Trips

We have trips almost every weekend during the semester, as well as fall and spring break trips and the annual Hut

Trip during winter break. For more trip details, visit our Trips page.We also hold Social Hour every Wednesday

or Thursday at 8:30pm virtually, this is a great way to meet other members!
